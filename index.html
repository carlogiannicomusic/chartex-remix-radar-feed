What it is

Spot it local. Remix it global.

Remixes are the fastest way for a producer to gain reach — but only if you catch a track while it's rising, not after it peaks. The signal is out there: TikTok creates, Spotify streams, YouTube views, Shazam tags. It's just scattered across platforms, and the professional tools are expensive, B2B, and built for labels.

Remix Radar pulls it all into one daily view, ranked for remix potential — momentum, freshness and cross-platform pickup. Open it with your morning coffee, find the track that's about to blow up, and start your edit before everyone else does. A Digital Renaissance instrument for producers, students and the curious.

“Think local, act global.” The same idea, pointed at sound: catch what's breaking anywhere, and make it yours.
